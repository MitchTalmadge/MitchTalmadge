Why Blue River?

We're a community first — small classes (16:1 student-to-faculty ratio), practical advising, and tuition that's a fraction of a four-year university.

$132/credit

In-state tuition for 2025–2026 — about a third of Oregon's public university rate. Most students complete an associate degree for under $9,000 total.

87% job placement

Among graduates of career-technical programs within 12 months of completion. Over 240 regional employers partner with our programs.

3 campuses + online

Main campus in Cascadia, plus satellite campuses in Riverside and Maple Grove. Half of all credit courses are available fully online.
